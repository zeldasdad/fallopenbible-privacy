# Privacy Policy

**Fall Open Bible**  
Last updated: May 1, 2026

## Summary

Fall Open Bible does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your reading activity stays on your device.

## Data Collection

We do not collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Device identifiers

## Data Storage

The only data stored on your device is your preferred Bible version (so the app remembers your choice). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

Scripture content is provided by API.Bible (American Bible Society). The scripture text is bundled within the app and accessed offline. No network requests are made during normal use of the app.

## Children's Privacy

Fall Open Bible does not knowingly collect any data from children or adults. No data is collected from any user.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this document and the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: fallopenbible@gmail.com
